Krishna Vallabhaneni President

Krishna Vallabhaneni (KV for short) has been an active member of his regional TSA chapter since his freshman year at Fox Chapel. Since then he has gradually stepped up the ladder and became the Vice President his junior year and now is the president his senior year at Fox Chapel. Krishna has competed in a number of state events: Webmaster, Tech Bowl, VEX, Video Game Design, Digital Video Production and Music Production and Open Source Software Development.

"KV shows excellent leadership skills and has the qualities to become a great leader"

Ken Grimm, Advisor

He currently plays tennis for Fox Chapel and is also the captain of the school Cricket team. Some of his hobbies include table tennis, watching movies and hanging out with friends. Next year Krishna is going to attend Pennsylvania State University for higher studies in the field of MIS (Management Information Systems). KV quotes "TSA has been a large part of my life and it has done nothing but help and advance my future career plans". He states that joining TSA was the best choice he made in his high school career and would love to keep on contributing to the cause even later in his life.
